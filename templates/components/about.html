Who We Are

Wellness Solutions is a leading wellness provider located at Western Heights, 5th Floor, Karuna Road, Westlands, Nairobi. Through our specialized division, Japheth's Powered Fitness (JPF), we offer comprehensive health solutions to improve health outcomes and reduce healthcare costs for organizations and individuals alike.

We believe a healthy workforce is a productive workforce. Our wellness programs address rising workplace health challenges, improving physical and mental well-being to enhance productivity and lower insurance claims and absenteeism.

With the rise in lifestyle diseases, we focus on assessing risks through health surveys, ergonomic consultations, and health risk assessments. Our customized solutions aim to mitigate healthcare costs and improve overall well-being.

Collaborating with organizations such as Antarc, we offer comprehensive wellness programs, rehabilitation initiatives, and team-building exercises at various premium locations across Nairobi.

Our services, trusted by numerous organizations across Kenya, include biometric tests, ergonomic assessments, stress management, and workplace wellness consulting.
